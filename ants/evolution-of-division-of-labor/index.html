Evolution of division of labor

Ant worker traits are remarkable in that they evolve despite the fact that the workers themselves do not reproduce. As a part of our ongong collaboration with the Linksvayer Lab, in a recently published paper in eLife we showed that the evolution of genes involved in social behavior is constrained by the effects of gene co-expression networks. You can listen to a podcast where Tim Linksvayer describes this work here.
